About this Tool

An interactive Network+ exam preparation tool covering all 26 protocols listed in CompTIA N10-008 Domain 1.5 (Common ports and protocols, their application, and encrypted alternatives).

Built for active recall. Use the table for fast lookup, the family view to group what you've learned, the twin pairs to drill the most-tested pattern, flashcards for spaced repetition, and the lightning round to test under time pressure — exactly how the exam will feel.

Study Strategy

· Day 1: Master Table + By Family. Get the layout in your head.

· Day 2: Encrypted Twins. These are the highest-yield exam points.

· Day 3+: Flashcards daily until you can clear the deck without missing more than 2.

· Exam week: Lightning Round every day until you score 20+ in 60 seconds.
